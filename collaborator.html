  • Nicholas Beauchamp     Bio
    • dnb2389@columbia.edu
      Personal Site

    • Nicholas Beauchamp (Ph.D., New York University, expected 2012) teaches in the Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences program as well as POLS 4360, Mathematical Methods for Political Science. His research centers on political persuasion, language, and ideology, and employs automated text analysis and machine learning techniques to develop new ways of understanding the connections between speech, belief, and political action.
